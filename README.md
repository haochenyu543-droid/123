# Initial Backlog Ideas

**Project:** CampusConnect – AI-Powered Orientation & Campus Life Assistant Chatbot  
**Last Updated:** 18 May 2026  
**Status:** Initial Rough Backlog (to be refined in Sprint Planning)

---

## 🎯 Product Vision
A friendly, intelligent chatbot that helps new students navigate orientation, campus life, and social integration smoothly.

---

## 📋 User Stories

### 1. Orientation Information Support
- **As a new student**, I want to ask about the **orientation schedule** so that I know what events are happening each day.  
- **As a new student**, I want to get **detailed event information** (time, venue, description, what to bring) so I can prepare in advance.  
- **As a new student**, I want to ask **“How do I get to [location]?”** and receive clear directions or a map link.  
- **As a new student**, I want the bot to explain **important policies and procedures** (attendance, dress code, emergency contacts, etc.).

### 2. Campus Navigation & Practical Help
- **As a student**, I want to find **facilities** (library, canteen, printing room, medical room, etc.) quickly.  
- **As a student**, I want **transportation information** (bus routes, MRT, campus shuttle) from and to campus.  
- **As an international student**, I want help with **basic campus services** (SIM card, bank, accommodation, etc.).

### 3. Social Engagement & Ice-breakers
- **As a new student**, I want the bot to **suggest ice-breaker games** and group activities suitable for my orientation group.  
- **As a new student**, I want **personalised activity recommendations** based on my interests (sports, music, coding, arts, etc.).  
- **As a shy student**, I want gentle suggestions on **how to make friends** and join social events.  
- **As a student**, I want the bot to help me **find students with similar interests** for study groups or meetups.

### 4. Academic & Student Life Support
- **As a freshman**, I want to ask about **course registration, faculty contacts, and academic resources**.  
- **As a student**, I want recommendations for **clubs and societies** that match my hobbies.  
- **As a student**, I want quick answers about **examination schedules, assignment deadlines, and important academic dates**.

### 5. Feedback & Continuous Improvement
- **As a user**, I want to **give quick feedback** (👍 / 👎 + comment) after each conversation so the bot can improve.  
- **As Student Affairs staff**, I want to see **usage statistics and most frequently asked questions** in a simple dashboard.

---

## 📌 Additional Rough Ideas (Future Epics)

- Multi-language support (English + Mandarin + Malay + Tamil)
- Integration with university single sign-on (SSO)
- Push notifications for important events
- Voice input support
- Integration with campus map API
- Admin panel for content updates without coding
